What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science focused on building systems that can perform tasks that usually require human intelligence.

This includes abilities like: learning, understanding language, recognizing images, decision-making, and more.

  • 🧠 Machine Learning (ML) – computers learn from examples
  • 💬 Natural Language Processing (NLP) – understanding text and speech
  • 👁️ Computer Vision – recognizing images and video
  • 🎯 Decision Making – analyzing information and recommendations
